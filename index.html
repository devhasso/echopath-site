What's Inside

Built for Focus & Flow

Every element of Echo Path is designed to put you in a calm, focused state — then challenge your precision.

Precision Drawing

Watch the echo path animate, then reproduce it as accurately as possible. Scored on accuracy, speed, and fluidity across 100 handcrafted levels — with more levels planned.

Star Ratings

Earn up to 3 stars per level. Chase perfect scores, climb the rankings, and push your accuracy to the limit.

32 Achievements

Unlock 32 achievements as you progress — from your first completed level to win streaks, perfect runs, and speed milestones.

Daily Challenge

A new unique path every day. Complete it for triple coin rewards and keep your daily streak alive. Miss a day, start again.

Power-Ups

Use Path Echo to reveal the ghost trail, Slow Motion to buy thinking time, Time Extend for extra seconds, or Shield to protect your hearts under pressure.

Trail Skins

Customise your drawing trail with unlockable colour skins — Fire, Cosmic, Ice, Gold, Cyan, Rainbow, and more. Make your path uniquely yours.

Hearts & Energy

You have 5 hearts to spend. Lose one on every failed attempt — refill by watching a short ad, spending gems, or waiting for them to regenerate.

Coming Soon
Leaderboard

Compete with players worldwide. Your total stars will determine your global ranking — climb to the top and hold your position.

Cloud Save

Sign in with Google to sync your progress across devices. Your achievements, stars, gems, and coins always follow you.

Statistics

Track your accuracy trends, total stars earned, win streaks, levels completed, and more in the detailed stats screen.

In-Game Shop

Spend coins and gems on power-ups, trail skins, heart refills, and gem packs. Earn coins by playing — or top up with a purchase when you need them fast.

Ad-Free Option

Play completely uninterrupted with a one-time Ad-Free purchase. No banners, no interruptions — just the game, the path, and your focus.
