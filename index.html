Gameplay

How to Play

Simple to pick up, satisfying to master.

1
Watch

The echo path animates on screen. Study its shape, speed, and direction carefully.

2
Remember

The path disappears. Hold the shape in your mind before the timer runs out.

3
Trace

Draw the path from memory as accurately as you can. Every pixel counts.

4
Score

Get scored on accuracy. Earn stars, collect coins, and unlock the next challenge.
